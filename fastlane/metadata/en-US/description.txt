Ever been in someone's house, or in a party, and wanted to play Sueca, but you didn't have playing cards?

This app is perfect for you! Now you can play Sueca with no playing cards! You just need to sort the cards and have fun drinking with your friends!

Play with the rules that we already provide (you can even learn how to play if you don't know how to yet), or setup your own cards with your own rules!

Attention: you must have legal drinking age to use this app.
Drink with responsibility.
